Title:First Kiss
Author:lena1987Word Count:100Rating:GDisclaimer:Characters belong to Jane Austen.First Kiss
Her face is tilted up to mine,and her dark eyes are wide. I swallow,finally laying my palm on her cheek. Her flesh is soft and warm.
"Marianne," I whisper hoarsely,entranced.
The garden is quiet:we are utterly alone.
"Brandon," she says breathlessly. Then,daringly:"Christopher."
I close my eyes,a groan falling from my mouth that is full of longing and desire. My name from her lips is surely a sound from heaven.
"Yes?" I ask,bending closer.
"Oh," she murmurs,her fingers now curling into my coat. "Yes."
Heart racing,I press my lips to hers.
